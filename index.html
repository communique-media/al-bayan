Al-Bayan

A support initiative for emerging independent journalists, forging solidarity from Brussels to Gaza.

Al-Bayan aims to platform a new generation of independent journalists in Gaza, defying censorship and erasure to bring the voices of Palestinians under siege to the world—with reporting published and translated in both Arabic and English.
